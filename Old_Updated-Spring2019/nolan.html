Nolan Speice

Nolan 'the socks' Speice has been playing ultimate since the day hecame to Clemson as a first semester freshman. He found out ultimate existed and was instantly a frisbee baller for life. Nolan swam for his high school's swimming team and was a sprinter on his high school track team. Did I mention Nolan is real fast? He is. Other than burning people both on the track and in the water, Nolan played soccer, shot some hoops, and stomped colons all throughout his childhood. Something almost too interesting about Nolan, gentleman, grab your women, is that he can do the wave with his stomach. Nolan will often agree with someone by saying, "Yes, Sherlock Holmes cases may be the greatest set of stories ever written." Nolan is also a firm believer that the frisbee team should come onto the field to a song, commenting that "somebody work on that." In short, Nolan is 170 pounds of Irish RAGE. Notre Dame, watch out.
