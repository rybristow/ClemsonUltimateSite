Fall Ultimate is Underway!

 

Welcome to the official website of Clemson Ultimate.  We are just getting our fall season underway and this is where you can find out all you’ll need to know.  Important details:

 

Practice:  Mondays and Thursdays 7-10pm, starting on August 21 at the Club Fields

               

Map to Club Fields: MAP

 

                What to bring:  -  Dark colored shirt

-      White shirt

-      Cleats

-      WATER!

-      Good disc (if you have one, but not necessary)

All levels of players Welcome!

 

Fall League:  Come by the first practices to get registered for Fall League.

       

                        T-shirts for your fall league team will be $10

 

                        League play will start on Thursday, August 28

               

 

If you have any questions about practices, fall league, or in general contact the club president Jonathan Oliver, jholive@clemson.edu
