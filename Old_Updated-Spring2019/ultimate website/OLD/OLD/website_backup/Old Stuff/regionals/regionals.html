General Information

The 2000 Atlantic Coast Regional Championships were held on April 29th & 30th in Clemson, SC. Regionals was a strong tournament showcasing some of the best Ultimate in the country. UNC took first place, NC State took second, and UGA took third. To see the final results, click here.

Thanks to Alan Hoyle, David Silbey, Will Deaver, Mike Gerics, and Charles Kerr for organizing and observing the games. Thanks also to Tim Halt, our Regional Coordinator, for setting up the brackets.
