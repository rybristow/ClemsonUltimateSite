Directions

From the North From Atlanta/South
Find your way to I-85 South
Take I-85 South until SC exit 19B, this is Hwy. 76/28
Continue on Hwy. 76/28 until the exit for Clemson
The Clemson exit is the intersection of Hwy. 93 & Hwy. 76
Continue on Hwy. 93, bare left at the 4th light, there will be a Subway on the right
Continue until you see the Fields on the left, about 1 mile
If you get lost, ask someone where the "Baseball Field" is

Take I-85 North until SC exit 19B, this is Hwy. 76/28
