Jordan "JMoney" Monroe

The first thing you need to know about JMoney is that the dude knows how to thrift. I'm not just talking about your run-of-the-mill Salvation Army or Goodwill, Jordan knows where every obscure thrift shop within a 40 mile radius of Clemson is located. There is no one better at finding insane deals than him, I can attest to this as I witnessed him buy an entire couch for $5. Yes a $5 couch that now resides in the Wigwam.
