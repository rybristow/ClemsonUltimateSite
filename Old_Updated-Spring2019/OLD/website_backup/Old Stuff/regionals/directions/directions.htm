Directions

2000 Atlantic Coast Regionals will be played on the Club Sports Fields (Fields 1-6 on the Map below), and the Intramural Fields (Fields 13-18 on the Map below). The Captain's meeting will be held at the tent, which will be located on the Club Sports Fields. You can park outside of the fence around the Club Sports Fields, and you can park along the road in the spots when playing at the Intramural Fields.

From the NorthFrom Atlanta/South
  • Find your way to I-85 South
  • Take I-85 South until SC exit 19B, this is Hwy. 76/28
  • Continue on Hwy. 76/28 until the exit for Clemson
  • The Clemson exit is the intersection of Hwy. 93 & Hwy. 76
  • Continue on Hwy. 93, bare left at the 4th light, there will be a Subway on the right
  • Continue until you see the Fields on the left, about 1 mile
  • If you get lost, ask someone where the "Baseball Field" is
  • Get on I-85 North
  • Take I-85 North until SC exit 19B, this is Hwy. 76/28
  • Continue on Hwy. 76/28 until the exit for Clemson
  • The Clemson exit is the intersection of Hwy. 93 & Hwy. 76
  • Continue on Hwy. 93, bare left at the 4th light, there will be a Subway on the right
  • Continue until you see the Fields on the left, about 1 mile
  • If you get lost, ask someone where the "Baseball Field" is
