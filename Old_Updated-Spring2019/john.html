John "Gypsy" Lipsey

What can I say about John that these pictures don't already say? He would gladly help your grandmother across the street, he's probably a blackbelt in boy scouts by now, and he is as mean and cruel as a baby panda. Don't let this smile fool you though when on the field the Gypsy will shed no tears for those who are unfortunate enough to be matched up with him. With a sports background of Tennis, School, and Church Basketball John is ready to get up in your face if he has to. John has been quoted as saying "I will take the road less traveled" and I can only imagine that he meant while you are running for the disc he will just take to the sky and grab it.
