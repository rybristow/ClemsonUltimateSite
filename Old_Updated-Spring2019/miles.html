Miles "Big Nuts" McDonald

The Chattanooga, TN boy who is downright nasty to defenses when handling, Miles McDonald has been playing frisbee since there was frisbee. However, he began playing organised ultimate frisbee in 2004 at his local, Mcallie, high school. His team was actually quite good and they went to High School Easterns, Padeia Cup, and even Terminus. Miles was even the captain of the team his Junior and Senior Years of high school. Miles' favorite throw is his "slap yo mutha nasty" low release flick and his "cherry on top" high release flick. When Miles gets older, he plans on proudly being a ninth generation Tennessee farm owner. When he dies, he wants to handle on a never-ending zone point and make defensive players look stupid for the rest of eternity. In a final word from Miles, "They call me Big Nuts.".
