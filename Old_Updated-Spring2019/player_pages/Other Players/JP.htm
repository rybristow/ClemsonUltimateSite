Jonathan Plyler
J.P. is big. After sitting out of the college scene for a couple of years, he has come back to play for Clemson for his senior season. J.P. played ultimate in 2005-2007 for Winthrop University. After his sophomore year, he transferred to Clemson. Due to time constraints and injuries he was not able to play last year for the joint chiefs. We are excited to have J.P. back and have the average height of our team raised a couple inches. J.P.'s sports background is interesting as he was a curler in his younger years. His favorite throw is either the IO long bomb flick, or the mythical helicopter throw. Thought to be found only in fairy tales and ancient greece, J.P. brings the helicopter to life, and it is both devastatingly and deliciously good. An interesting life experience J.P. had was when he was in the ninth grade he attended a Britney Spears concert on April 6th, 2000. But this was to be a Britney Spears concert like no other he had ever attended before. At this concert, J.P. won the "Why should Britney Spears date you contest" and Britney sang to J.P. on stage at the Bi-Lo center in Greenville. If that isn't enough, J.P. also has the ability to call in his secret weapon, nuclear missile strike.
