Niles Brinton
Currently playing for the UC Santa Barbara Black Tide, Niles Brinton remains a powerhouse of motivation, inspiration, and trash-talking for the Joint Chiefs. Clemson's 2012 Callahan and All-Region Nominee, Brinton's playing style has gradually progressed from simple and cautious to one that made Chuck Norris describe him as "one of the few men in the universe with balls the size of my own." When not skying fools, getting layout D's, throwing no-look hucks or shredding zones, Brinton's 6 feet, 160 lb. frame can be found gallivanting around town on one of his incredibly badass bicycles, eating unleavened bread, and frequenting roadside produce stands. Previous nicknames include "Baddest White Dude Ever" and "Khai Nui," which is Thai for Gorilla Dick. Brinton is also frequently seen guiding, counseling, and advising his team protégé, the young William Gordon. Gordon, when asked if he could sum up Niles Brinton in ten words or less, said "Absolutely not. Ten words isn't enough."
